IoT Pet Cage Smart Door System with Food and Water Releaser Using Arduino

The IoT Pet Cage Smart Door System with Food and Water Releaser Using Arduino; simply called “smart pet cage” was built suitable for use of pets especially cats and dogs. This project works, by turning on and waiting for the system to initialize. After that, the automatic door, and the food and water releaser will be ready for use. The food and water are stored in a separate containers. The servo motor serves as the tool for opening or releasing the food from the container, while the water pump replenishes water in the container if it is already at low level. The project implements IoT, which enables a pet owner to monitor the level of the stored water and food in the container.
